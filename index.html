We believe in technology as a force for good.
We have one mission. To turn technology into a tool for social change. The blockchain is revolutionizing money, businesses and organizations. Our idea is simple: we want to revolutionize charity.
Become part of a network of heroes.
So how does it work? Instead of donating money or time, you help us by donating a tiny bit of your computer power. We use this power to mine cryptocoins (think Bitcoin), and then give it all away. It's more than just a dashboard. It's people helping people.
Blocks for Change is a beautiful dashboard for Chrome. It replaces your boring new tab page with a gorgeous, inspiring dashboard. Every tab you open a new tab, you also donate a tiny bit of CPU power to help us mine cryptocoin for charity.

Got questions? We're here to answer.
Use the arrows to browse or FAQs section. If you really want to talk to us don't hesitate to get in touch. If you want to be part of the community, join our Slack channel by clicking on the icon on the bottom right corner to get in touch directly with our team, or shoot us a message!
Who does the money go to?
We are working hard to look for partner charitable organizations to donate the funds to. Our goal is to partner with smaller organizatinos who have more difficulties finding donations. If you are part of a charitable organization, join our Slack channel to get in touch! Right now, we do not have any costs, so we donate 100% of what is generated. We are also putting in a lot of effort to make the entire process as transparent as possible. The possibility for you to vote for projects based on how many tabs you have opened is also in the roadmap.


Will it harm my computer?
Blocks for Change will not harm your computer in any way. It uses a very small amount of CPU power (around 3-5% generally, 10% at most during peaks), so you should not even notice it's running. If you need to perform some CPU heavy actions, you can always turn mining off in the settings.


Do I have to pay to use it?
Nope. Downloading - and using - Blocks for Change, is free and always will be. Just by using it you will already be helping out! If you want to help the team, get in touch with us through Slack, and share the project. The more users we have the more people we'll be able to help.
